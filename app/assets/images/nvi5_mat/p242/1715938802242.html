PERIMETRO E AREA DEL RETTANGOLO

 

 

Perimetro
P = (b + h) × 2
Formule inverse:
b = (P : 2) – h
h = (P : 2) – b

 

Area
A = b × h
Formule inverse:
b = A : h
h = A : b
